Starve Sandwich

Homemade Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Sandwich Bread
  • Lettuce
  • Mayo
  • 1 slice of ham
  • (optional) Queso Fresco

Steps

  1. Place the beef patties on the stove and cook to perfection
  2. Lather bread slices with mayo
  3. (optional) Add Queso Fresco
  4. Lettuce goes above the ham
  5. Enjoy!
