Cheese Burger

Basic Cheese Burger

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Mayo
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Place the beef patties on the stove and cook to perfection
  2. Lather buns with mayo and place the other side on the stove to toast
  3. Place the cheese on the cooked burger patty and then build the burger
  4. Lettuce, Tomato, and Mustard go above the patty
  5. Enjoy!
